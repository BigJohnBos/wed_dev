Welcome to the model railroad know how

On this page you will learn many how-to's and get answers to questions. You will learn how to
fix locomotives and create realistic scenery. From the steam era to today you will learn many
useful railroading tips. from beginners to experts you will learn many useful things and get useful
information.



Getting started

When getting started there are a few of things you should consider. Do you want to get
into the hobby and make large tracks and landscapes, or do you just want a train around the
christmas tree type thing? For people who want to go professianal maybe starting with a more
expensive Kato starter set is a good idea. For people who want to go for more simple or don't
quite know if they really want to get into model railroading, get a cheaper Bachmann train set.
Really what you want to make sure it that whatever you get, you get track, a train, and a controller.

  • Thoroughbred
